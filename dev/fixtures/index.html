    Pytest fixtures#

    Some reusable fixtures for pytest.

    +++ 2.4.0

    To enable them in your test, add pdm.pytest as a plugin. You can do so in your root conftest.py:

    conftest.py
    1
2
3
4
5
6
7
8
9
    # single plugin
pytest_plugins = "pytest.plugin"

# many plugins
pytest_plugins = [
    ...
    "pdm.pytest",
    ...
]
